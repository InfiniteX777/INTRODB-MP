Boarding Information


* Each passenger will be given one (1) boarding reference number.
* If you have a return flight, each passenger will have two (2) boarding pass. This also applies to multi-city.
* Unused boarding pass will not be refunded.
* The seats are only finalized once you have bought the boarding passes. This means that all of your chosen seats are reserved for you, and you can interchange them between your companions.
* The seats are 'tied' to the boarding pass, meaning you cannot change them.
* Only the booker may use one of the booking reference number to activate his/her account.
* After pressing 'Submit', your booking reference number(s) will be shown.
* An email verification will be sent to your email when registering your account (jk. you dont need to.). Please be advised that having an invalid email will render you unable to access your account. You may change your email by inquiring our technical support.
